                No, not the World Cup &mdash; we&rsquo;re talkin&rsquo; good ol&rsquo; American college football. After all, as the homepage of the <a href="http://www.al.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">The Huntsville (Alabama) Times</a> will tell you, kickoff is only a short 68 days away!
