<a href="http://nutshellmail.com/">NutshellMail</a>, the site that "<a href="http://nutshellmail.com/about/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">transforms your primary email account into a universal inbox so you can retrieve messages and manage all your accounts in one place</a>," has added the ability to post status updates to MySpace via email according to their newsletter sent out today.  Nutshell is interesting as it centralizes all of your social sites to your inbox.  It currently supports Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Gmail, Yahoo, and many <a href="http://nutshellmail.com/supported_services/email/">others</a>.  The service is free and seems to be expanding it's services pretty frequently.

<p />

I would love to see some further integration with Twitter status updates (like Topify) and Facebook but [A]ll in all it's a pretty solid way to archive and maintain your online presence.
