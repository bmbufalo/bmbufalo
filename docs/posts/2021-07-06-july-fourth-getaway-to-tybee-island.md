<p>We escaped out to Tybee Island for the Fourth of July weekend and had a wonderful time! It was our first time out visiting and there was so much to see and do we are hoping to go again and explore, especially Savanah, Georgia since we only got a taste of the beautiful architecture and history. </p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

<h2>Beaches and dolphins</h2>
